📖 What is Product Management?

Product Management is the strategic function responsible for defining, developing, and launching successful products. Product Managers understand customer needs, define product vision, prioritize features, and work with cross-functional teams (engineering, design, marketing, sales) to deliver value to users and achieve business goals. Business Analysts bridge the gap between business problems and technical solutions by gathering requirements, analyzing data, and ensuring project success.

🛣️ Complete Learning Roadmap

1. Learn Business Fundamentals
Understand how businesses operate, revenue models, market dynamics, and basic economics.
2. Learn SQL & Excel
SQL for data querying, Excel for analysis and reporting. Essential for data-driven decisions.
3. Understand Product Lifecycle
Learn the stages of product development from ideation to launch to retirement.
4. Improve Communication Skills
Effective communication is key for stakeholder management, presentations, and team collaboration.
5. Learn Agile & Scrum
Agile methodology and Scrum framework for product development and project management.
6. Build Portfolio & Case Studies
Create product requirement documents, user stories, and real-world case studies.
7. Interview Preparation & Job Applications
Prepare for product management interviews, build resume, and apply to companies.
