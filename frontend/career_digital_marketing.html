📖 About This Career Path

Digital Marketing is the promotion of brands, products, and services using digital channels like search engines, social media, email, and websites. It's one of the fastest-growing fields with endless opportunities. HR (Human Resources) focuses on recruiting, training, employee relations, and building company culture. Entrepreneurship is about starting and scaling your own business venture, solving real problems, and creating value. These roles focus on business growth, people management, and creating value — offering diverse career paths for creative and ambitious individuals.

🛣️ Complete Learning Roadmap

1. Learn SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
Master the art of ranking websites on Google. Learn keyword research, on-page SEO, off-page SEO, and technical SEO.
2. Social Media Marketing (SMM)
Learn to build brand presence on Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and emerging platforms. Create engaging content and run ads.
3. Content Creation & Copywriting
Create compelling content for blogs, videos, emails, and social media. Master the art of persuasive writing.
4. Email Marketing & Automation
Learn to build email lists, create campaigns, and automate marketing workflows using tools like Mailchimp and HubSpot.
5. Google Ads & PPC
Master Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and other paid advertising platforms to drive targeted traffic.
6. Analytics & Data Insights
Learn to use Google Analytics, track campaign performance, and make data-driven marketing decisions.
7. Build Portfolio & Freelance/Job Apply
Create real projects, build your portfolio, and start applying for jobs or freelancing opportunities.
